Structural Plenitude Theory (SPT)

Vacuum as a viscoelastic medium

SPT proposes that the “vacuum” is not empty, but a pleromic substrate with viscoelastic properties. Light, gravitation and cosmological redshift emerge as dissipative and tension phenomena in this medium, instead of mere geodesics in a geometric space-time.

In this framework, objects such as 3I/ATLAS, planetary plasma tails and field anomalies cease to be exceptions and become dynamic signatures of the coupling between matter and Pleroma.

Model: SPT v1.3–1.4
Domain: Cosmology & gravitation
